Flinders University New City Campus

Flinders University's new city campus at Festival Plaza was opened in 2024. Located in the heart of the Adelaide CBD and cultural district the new campus features state-of-the-art teaching, meeting and student spaces.

The campus is adjacent to the Adelaide Railway Station and is at the centre of the hustle and bustle and the vibrant arts culture of the CBD.

Train, tram and bus services are all within easy reach, allowing visitors to explore the city centre and beyond, connection to our Tonsley and Bedford Park campuses via a door-to-door rail link.
