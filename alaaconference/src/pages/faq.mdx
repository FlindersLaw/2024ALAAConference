# Frequently Asked Questions


<details>
<summary>
Where will the 2024 ALAA Conference be held?
</summary>
The 2024 Australasian Legal Academics Association conference will be held at Flinders University's
[new city campus](https://www.flinders.edu.au/festival-plaza).
Opened only in February 2024 the new campus is located at the heart of Adelaide's
CBD and is adjacent to the Adelaide Railway startTransition, the Festival Centre and Parliament House.
</details>

<details>
<summary>
When will the 2024 ALAA Conference be held?
</summary>
The 2024 Australasian Legal Academics Association conference will be held from
July 3 - 5 2024.
</details>

<details>
